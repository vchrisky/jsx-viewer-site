JSX Viewer

Preview React .jsx & .tsx files — fully offline

Coming soon to Google Play

Render components straight from your phone

JSX Viewer opens .jsx and .tsx files from your device and renders them as live React components — entirely on-device. Babel and React are bundled inside the app, so it compiles and previews with no server, no network, and no setup. It even works in airplane mode.
